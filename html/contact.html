Cliniface is free and open source software. It was developed to connect the needs of patients and medical professionals with the insights and achievements of researchers. If your work is related to facial measurement and analysis, then integrating it into Cliniface will widen its exposure and allow it to help real people with real medical conditions. We are eager to collaborate with those who see the potential to extend Cliniface in new and exciting directions. Contact us now to discuss how we can build the future of 3D facial analysis together.

Selecting Languages

You can control the loading of hyphenation patterns with the help of MiKTeX Options.

Click Start → Programs → MiKTeX 2.9 → Maintenance → Settings to open the MiKTeX Options window (see Figure 4.1, “MiKTeX Options: General Settings”).

Click on the Languages tab. You will presented with the list of available languages (Figure 4.2, “MiKTeX Options: Languages”). Mark the languages, whose hyphenation patterns are to be loaded by the TeX engines.
